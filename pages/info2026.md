# Parent Pack for LiveWires 2026


## Key information

- **Dates:** 9–16 August 2026
- **Location:** Chafyn Grove School, Salisbury
- **Arrival:** Sunday 9 Aug, 4:30–5:30pm
- **Departure:** Sunday 16 Aug, 10:00–10:30am
- **Contact:** 020 3397 1111 / [livewires@scriptureunion.org.uk](mailto:livewires@scriptureunion.org.uk)


## Getting to LiveWires

LiveWires is held at Chafyn Grove School on the edge of Salisbury.

Arrival time: **Sunday 9th August, 4:30–5:30pm**. Please do not arrive before this time as we may not be ready.

Address for sat nav: [Chafyn Grove School, 33 Bourne Ave, Salisbury SP1 1LR](https://what3words.com/many.small.discrepancy)

We can also collect young people from:

* Salisbury train station (SAL)
* Millstream Coach Park

Please complete the [travel form](/travel) so we can arrange this.


## Leaving LiveWires

Departure is **Sunday 16th August**, between **10:00–10:30am**.

We can also provide drop-off at Salisbury train station or coach park if required.

Please ensure prompt collection as the team will be clearing the site afterwards.

## Keeping in touch

If you need to get in touch before or during the holiday, you can:

* **Phone:** 020 3397 1111.
* **Email:** [livewires@scriptureunion.org.uk](mailto:livewires@scriptureunion.org.uk).

The phone number always diverts to a leader’s mobile during the holiday. Reception is patchy so we may not respond immediately, but please leave a message.

## Allergies

We have participants with severe nut allergies. Please **do not** bring any nuts or nut products.

## Day out

The holiday includes a day out to a local attraction, which this year is expected to be an outdoor pursuits centre. Leaders will be supervising the youngsters at all times. They aren’t allowed off-site apart from this trip.

## Mobile phones

Young people may bring mobile phones, but use is limited. Phones are not allowed in dormitories overnight or at mealtimes. Please do not expect regular contact during the week.

## Money

Bringing money is optional!

There will be a tuck shop, bookshop and merchandise available during the week.

We use a cashless system. Any money can either be:
* paid in advance before the holiday, or
* handed in on arrival for safekeeping

Some electronics projects have an additional cost. We will email full details in advance if this applies.

Further information will be sent closer to the holiday.

## Insurance

LiveWires is fully insured against accidents where Scripture Union is legally responsible. If you would like additional cover (e.g. personal belongings), please arrange this separately.

## What to pack

### Clothing
* Enough clothes for 8 days
* Smart outfit for final night
* Old clothes for messy/wet activities
* Raincoat
* Sun hat and sun cream
* Swimwear
* Sports shoes + spare footwear

### Essentials
* Wash kit and towels
* Bedding (pillow, pillowcase, duvet/sleeping bag, sheets)
* Reusable water bottle
* Pens

### Activities
* Day bag
* Lunchbox (for day out)
* Bible (modern translation if possible)
* Musical instruments (optional)

### Money and admin
* Money for electronics projects (if applicable)
* Money for tuck shop / bookshop / merch
* Train/bus tickets for the return journey

### Medical
* Any medication (including inhalers + spares, travel sickness etc.)
* **NO NUTS**
